Trigslink

Founders: Aakash Jaiswal & Gianluca Godfrey

Comprehensive Market Infrastructure

Trigslink is a decentralized protocol designed for instant 15-second settlement. By utilizing AI and Chainlink infrastructure, the Trigslink SDK allows developers to build markets with machine-verified finality and unified liquidity.

Why Trigslink?

Traditional markets suffer from a 'Zombie Period' where capital is locked. Trigslink eliminates this through the Universal Liquidity Rails (ULR) and Autonomous Grounding Layer.

  • 15-Second Autonomous Settlement
  • Universal Liquidity Rails (ULR)
  • Cross-Chain Developer SDK
  • Machine-Verified Truth Engine
